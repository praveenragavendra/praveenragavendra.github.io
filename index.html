About Me

Hello! I'm Praveen Ragavendra B, an Electrical and Electronics Engineering student at Mepco Schlenk Engineering College (Batch 2023-2027). I am passionate about building systems where **hardware meets intelligence** — combining core electrical engineering concepts with **AI, automation, and intelligent systems**. My goal is to engineer systems that make infrastructure **smarter, efficient, and intelligent**.
